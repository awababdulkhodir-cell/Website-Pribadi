About me

Hello, I'm Muichiro Tokito

Muichiro Tokito is the Mist Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, a title he earned despite being only 14 years old. He is a child prodigy of immense talent, mastering his sword skills and ascending to the rank of Hashira in a mere two months. Physically, Muichiro is a short, young man with long, straight black hair that fades to a turquoise color at the ends. He typically wears a loose-fitting, oversized version of the standard Demon Slayer uniform, which obscures his build, and he is often seen with a blank, listless expression.

Initially, Muichiro is portrayed as aloof, apathetic, and constantly lost in his own thoughts, often forgetting people and events. His mind tends to wander, making him seem disconnected from the world and the people around him. This detached personality is not his true nature but a result of profound amnesia, which he developed after a devastating personal tragedy. He operates purely on logic, lacking emotion and drive until his memories are reawakened.

Muichiro's tragic past is the key to his personality. He and his twin brother, Yuichiro, were orphaned and lived together until a brutal demon attack killed his brother and left Muichiro on the brink of death. This trauma suppressed his memories and ignited a deep-seated rage that propelled him into the Demon Slayer Corps. As the Mist Hashira, he is a master of the Mist Breathing style, and it is later revealed that he is a descendant of the legendary Upper Rank One demon, Kokushibo.

"Even if I lose my memory, my body will remember that anger that will not leave me until I die."
